Our Mission

SafeHaven is a community-driven safety platform designed to help individuals, especially women, navigate their surroundings with confidence. By reporting unsafe locations and accessing real-time alerts, users can make informed decisions and contribute to a safer environment.

Why Choose SafeHaven?

📍 Report Unsafe Locations

Users can mark areas they feel unsafe in, helping others stay informed.

🗺️ Interactive Safety Map

View reported areas in real-time using an integrated map powered by Leaflet.js.

🔐 Secure & Anonymous Reporting

Reports can be anonymous to protect user privacy while ensuring community safety.

🔔 Instant Alerts

Get instant notifications about unsafe locations in your area.
